Blackjack Rules

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2-10): Face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10 points
  • Aces: 1 or 11 points (whichever is better)

Game Flow

  1. Place your bet (minimum $15)
  2. Receive two cards face up
  3. Dealer receives one card face up, one face down
  4. Choose to Hit (take another card) or Stand (keep your hand)
  5. If you bust (go over 21), you lose immediately
  6. Dealer reveals hidden card and must hit on 16 or less

Blackjack

An Ace and a face card (or 10) on the first two cards is a Blackjack and pays 2:1!

Winning

  • If you have a higher hand than the dealer: You win even money (1:1)
  • If you have Blackjack: You win double (2:1)
  • If you tie with the dealer: Push (your bet is returned)
  • If you bust or dealer has higher: You lose your bet
